## WE ARE EXPERTS IN

### LIVE STREAMING

With our live streaming equipment it is possible to capture your special event live. It gives you a unique opportunity to share it with family, friends, work colleagues, and customers. Don't let travel expenses or work commitments get in your way of people being a part of your next event, let them be there from anywhere in the world. Can't watch live? Don't worry; you will also have the option to host your video online after the event so people can catch up even after the Livestream has finished.

### VIDEO RECORDING

Our video recording is the perfect opportunity to keep lasting memories for years to come. Whether you want us to record your wedding or a business conference, we can help.

IStream's staff members have years of experience in video recording and live streaming, and that is a guarantee we will capture every detail, and make sure the entire event is recorded professionally.

At the end of the event, you will receive the video on a USB stick ready to play on any computer or smart TV.

### REAL-TIME VIDEO FEEDS

Do you need to provide a real-time video feed of your event? It can be a perfect opportunity to stream live video to everyone in the venue even if they are in a different room.

Thanks to the real-time video feed, you can stream live video to big screens, or a small screen in another room. Live video feeds are the best way to ensure that everyone at your event gets up-close and clear images of what's happening.
